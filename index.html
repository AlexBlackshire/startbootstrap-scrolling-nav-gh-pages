About us

Our Values

Netglow was founded upon our undying passion for eSports and our strong ambition to advocate for the best talent within the professional gaming and streaming industries. We seek to bring brands together with influencers who create outstanding content through services such as YouTube and Twitch.

Our Vision

We envision a team of intelligent, hard-working, dedicated, and well-rounded young adults who share a love for all things gaming. This vision is being made into a reality, we know the talent exists. We're just waiting to represent it.

Our Mission

We strive to build the best partnerships for our talent. Our brand seeks to brighten the spotlight on our talent's character and stories, so that their worldwide audience can bloom and grow.
